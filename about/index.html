About

I’m a Ph.D. candidate in Computational Intelligence and Systems Science from Tokyo Institute of Technology with an interest in combining machine learning techniques with real-world applications, embedded systems and the Internet of things (IoT). For 5 years, I have been applying machine learning techniques, both supervised and unsupervised, to learn and recognize social touch actions between humans and a soft-stuffed robot. I also developed a soft and flexible fabric-based touch sensing system specially for the soft-stuffed robot to sense touch.

When I’m not doing any researches, I love to go out and discover new delicious food and a fresh cup of coffee. On a cloudy day, I also love to spent times doing any handcraft hobbies and going through the stack of books at home.

If you would like to learn more, please get in touch!
